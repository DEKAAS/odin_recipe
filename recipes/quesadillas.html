These Quesadillas Are Made with Interstellar Ingredients

--Ingridients--

  • 8 flour tortillas
  • 2 cups shredded cheese
  • 1 cup cooked and diced chicken
  • 1/2 cup diced pineapple
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1/2 cup cooked macaroni and cheese
  • 1/2 cup crushed sweet and savory cereal (such as Chex Mix or Honey Bunches of Oats)
  • 1/4 cup crumbled cookies (such as Oreo or Nilla Wafers)
  • 1/4 cup pickled jalapeño slices
  • 1/4 cup sweet and sour sauce
  1. Start by preparing the fillings for the quesadillas. In separate bowls, combine the shredded cheese, cooked and diced chicken, diced pineapple, blueberries, cooked macaroni and cheese, crushed sweet and savory cereal, crumbled cookies, pickled jalapeño slices, and sweet and sour sauce. Mix well.

  2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat.

  3. Place a flour tortilla in the skillet and sprinkle a handful of shredded cheese on one half of the tortilla.

  4. Add your desired fillings on top of the cheese. Be careful not to overfill the quesadilla.

  5. Fold the other half of the tortilla over the fillings to form a half-moon shape.

  6. Cook the quesadilla for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until the tortilla is crispy and the cheese is melted.

  7. Repeat steps 3-6 with the remaining tortillas and fillings.Serve the quesadillas with your favorite dipping sauce and enjoy!
