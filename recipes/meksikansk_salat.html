This salad Will Make You Question Reality

--Ingridients--

  • 4 cups mixed greens
  • 1 pound cooked and sliced chicken
  • 1 cup diced pineapple
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 1 cup crushed sweet and savory cereal (such as Chex Mix or Honey Bunches of Oats)
  • 1/2 cup crumbled cookies (such as Oreo or Nilla Wafers)
  • 1/4 cup pickled jalapeño slices
  • 1/4 cup sweet and sour sauce
  1. Start by assembling the base of the salad. Add 4 cups of mixed greens to a large salad bowl.

  2. Add 1 pound of cooked and sliced chicken on top of the mixed greens.

  3. Add 1 cup of diced pineapple and 1 cup of blueberries to the salad.

  4. Add 1 cup of cooked macaroni and cheese to the salad.

  5. Sprinkle 1 cup of crushed sweet and savory cereal on top of the salad.

  6. Sprinkle 1/2 cup of crumbled cookies on top of the salad.

  7. Add 1/4 cup of pickled jalapeño slices to the salad.

  8. Drizzle 1/4 cup of sweet and sour sauce over the top of the salad.

  9. Add any optional fun ingredients from the list above, such as melted chocolate on the cookies or sliced bananas on top of the salad.

  10. Toss the salad well to evenly distribute all the ingredients. Serve and enjoy your fun and delicious salad!
